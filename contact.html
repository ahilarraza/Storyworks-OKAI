About this Storyworks project

What is VAYN?

VAYN is a Storyworks concept created for the Interactive Media Management program. It imagines how Innovate By Day could use an AI-powered conversational avatar to welcome new clients and transform fragmented ideas into structured project briefs.

The project explores UX design, user research, AI-assisted communication, and storytelling as tools for improving first contact between agencies and clients.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Make first contact with a marketing agency feel human, clear, and efficient.

Vision: A future where AI empowers creative teams—not replacing them—by gathering context, organizing information, and improving client readiness before the first meeting.
