The Problem

Most agencies still rely on static contact forms or generic chatbots. For busy business owners:

  • They don’t get quick, clear answers to basic questions.
  • Forms feel cold, mechanical, and time-consuming.
  • Important context is often missing from intake, causing rework.

Our Solution

OKAI turns that first touchpoint into a guided conversation that:

  • Answers FAQs in real time about services, process, and timelines.
  • Asks smart follow-up questions to fill gaps in the brief.
  • Outputs a clear summary for the IBD team, ready for next steps.

Why OKAI works for Innovate By Day

Story-first intake

OKAI doesn’t just collect data. It asks questions in a way that helps clients tell the story behind their project, not just fill out fields.

Time-saving for the team

By pre-structuring project briefs, the IBD team spends less time chasing missing info and more time on strategy and creative work.

More confident clients

Clients feel heard and guided instead of overwhelmed by long forms or confusing agency jargon.

How OKAI fits into the website flow

  1. Visitor lands on the IBD site and clicks “Talk to OKAI”.
  2. OKAI asks warm-up questions about goals, audience, and timelines.
  3. The avatar generates a mini-brief that goes straight to the IBD team.

Behind the scenes, OKAI can be customized to fit different service lines (campaigns, content, consulting) and adjust the questions it asks.

Over time, VAIN learns which questions lead to better projects by tracking what information the team uses the most. This turns every new conversation into a chance to refine IBD’s intake process and improve client satisfaction.
