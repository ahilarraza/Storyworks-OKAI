What OKAI can do

OKAI is designed as a Storyworks prototype to show how a conversational avatar can support Innovate By Day clients from the very first interaction.

Guided Q&A flow

Pre-built question paths adapt to the type of project: campaign, content strategy, social media, or video production.

Project brief generator

At the end of the conversation, OKAI summarizes key goals, audience, timelines, and budget range into a mini-brief.

Service-aware answers

OKAI can answer basic questions about IBD’s process, timelines, and collaboration style, reducing friction for new leads.
